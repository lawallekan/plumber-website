Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service?

We service the entire San Francisco Bay Area including San Francisco, Oakland, San Jose, and surrounding communities. Contact us to confirm we service your specific location.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes! We provide 24/7 emergency plumbing services. Call us anytime at (800) 555-1234 for immediate assistance with burst pipes, sewage backups, or any plumbing emergency.

What are your rates?

Our rates vary depending on the service needed. We offer transparent, upfront pricing with no hidden fees. Standard service calls start at $99, while emergency rates are slightly higher. Contact us for a free estimate.

Are your plumbers licensed and insured?

Absolutely. All our plumbers are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We maintain all necessary certifications and carry comprehensive liability insurance for your protection.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we stand behind all our work with a 1-year warranty on labor. Parts and fixtures carry manufacturer warranties which we can help you process if needed.

How quickly can you respond to service requests?

For emergency calls, we typically arrive within 60 minutes. Standard service appointments are usually available within 24 hours, often the same day.
